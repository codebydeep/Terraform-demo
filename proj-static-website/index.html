Top Tourist Destinations

Dwarka

One of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites and home to the famous Dwarkadhish Temple.

Somnath

Experience the grandeur of the historic Somnath Temple located along the Arabian Sea.

Girnar

A sacred mountain range offering breathtaking views, trekking routes and ancient temples.

Gir National Park

The only natural habitat of the Asiatic Lion and one of India's finest wildlife destinations.

Rann of Kutch

Witness the spectacular white desert and vibrant Rann Utsav celebrations.

Diu

Relax on pristine beaches and explore Portuguese-era architecture.

Why Visit Gujarat?

Gujarat offers a unique blend of spirituality, history, wildlife, culture and coastal beauty. From the sacred city of Dwarka to the majestic Girnar Hills and the famous Rann of Kutch, every destination tells a fascinating story.
